Air Fried Potatoes

Home

Desription

This recipe is great for a fast week night meal.

These potoatoes come out perfect crunchy and cripsy everytime! In only 25 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1lb of baby potatoes (I prefer baby red pototoes)
  • 1tsp seasoned salt
  • 2tsp Italino seasoning
  • 1tsp freshly cracked black pepper
  • 2tbsp olive oil
  • 1tsp onion powder
  • 1tsp garlic powder
  • 1 small bunch of parsley (optional)

Directions

  1. Cut all potatoes in half
  2. Finely chop parsley
  3. In a large bowl combine potatoes and spices
  4. Add olive oil to the bowl
  5. Mix until well coated with seasoning
  6. Preheat air fryer to 400 degrees for 5 minutes
  7. Add contents of bowl to air fryer basket and cook for 20-25 minutes
  8. Plate potatoes with some of the chopped parsley
  9. Serve immediately
