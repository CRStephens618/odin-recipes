Easy Pork Chop Brine

Home

Description

This recipe brings your pork chops from everyday to out of this world!!!

A quickly prepared brine followed by a few hours in the fridge leads to the juiciest pork chops EVER!!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of cold water
  • 1/3 cup of salt
  • 2tbsp of black pepper
  • 2tbsp of dark brown sugar
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 4 - 6 Pork chops, preferably thick cut
  • Zip top bag

Directions

  1. Combine water, salt, pepper, sugar, and bay leaves, mix well
  2. Add pork chops to zip top bag
  3. Pour brine solution into bag
  4. Remove as much air as possible
  5. Set in fridge for 4 - 5 hours.
  6. Cook however you prefer. Bar B Q works great!!
