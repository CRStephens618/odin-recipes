The PERFECT Bacon



Description

This recipe makes cooking bacon simple, quick and passive!

Almost all hands free except for putting into the oven!

Ingredients

  • Thick cut bacon
  • Cookie Sheet
  • Aluminum Foil

Directions

  1. Line cookie sheet with aluminum foil
  2. Place bacon on sheet in a single layer
  3. Place sheet in oven on middle rack
  4. Turn oven to 400 degrees farenheit
  5. Cook bacon for 25 minutes, flipping halfway
  6. Once complete, remove sheet from oven, and remove bacon to paper towel lined plate to drain for 2-3 minutes.
  7. Serve with eggs for breakfast or use on burgers and sandwiches for extra flavour!!
